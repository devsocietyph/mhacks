Partners

Mental Health Hacks wouldn't have been possible without the support of our partners. We're sharing with you the folks and organizations that assisted in making everything possible. Read about their advocacies and learn more about them — this is our community, our fight for better mental health.

Co-Presenters

The 2030 Youth Force in the Philippines is a community of youth advocating for the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals particularly in the Philippines.
YFPH is anchored to the 2030 Youth Force of the Asia-Pasific Region, organized by the United Nations Development Programme and United Nations Volunteers.

International Youth United (IYU) is a mission-based, and youth-led organization dedicated to enforce human rights, and ensure community development.

#MentalHealthPH is a social media advocacy that aims to create a safe community of people affected by mental health problems.
With volunteers from all over the country, #MentalHealthPH envisions an inclusive and empowered community for people affected by mental health problems.

The Youth for Mental Health Coalition is an organization that aims to promote mental health awareness in the Philippines. We aim to break the stigma on mental health by providing avenues that educate the masses, most especially the youth, about what mental health disorders really are and its situation here in the country. The organization composes of students from different colleges and universities, health professionals, and mental health advocates from all over the country.

Development Partner

YouthHack is a nonprofit organization that was started in 2014 with the goal of helping students learn more about technology, startups and entrepreneurship. Through a variety of events, workshops and programs, YouthHack has helped hundreds of students in the past year. We hope to continue building the startup ecosystem in underdeveloped communities by bringing together the bright young minds of tomorrow and providing them with the network, resources and mentors to help them turn their ideas into a reality.

Minor Development Partners

Pugad is the official literary and visual arts folio of the Ateneo de Manila Senior High School. Here in Pugad, we dedicate ourselves to creating art, whether it be literary or visual. To constantly improve our craft, we work to make sure that our members grow as artists, in both skill and self-confidence. We teach, inspire, and provide opportunities for them to create so that at the end of the day, they may share it with others.

The XS Next Lab explores the intersection of the arts, science, technology and design. The lab provides for formal research opportunities in a K-12 environment for both students and faculty of Xavier School.
