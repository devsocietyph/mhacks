You Are What You'll Be

Team Better Days

Have you forgotten where we will all return?

"You Are What You'll Be" is a collective of poetry from Team Better Days tackling different gender issues. From the gentleness of "warm like flowers in the summer" to the impetus of "I had not come out with the desire for Adam / and only Adam" -- words reap our very souls from these bodies.

Trigger Warning for the piece "A Note Before I Was Forced Out Of The Closet", involving violence, death, and blood.
