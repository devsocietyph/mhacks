Self-harm Song

Never-ending War

Noleil

I try to keep the chaos inside, but it gets out of hand.

Noleil's "Never-ending War" is a take on self-harm in soft melodic tunes, digging into self-harm in a confessional lull.

"Our song centers on how having mental health issues is like facing a constant and brutal war within. People are wrong when they think that those who do self-harm don't try to help themselves. They should never invalidate peoples feelings and judge people's actions because they don't know the kind of battles others are facing."
