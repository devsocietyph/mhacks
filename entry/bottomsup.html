Bottoms Up!

Mega Minds

We know "bottoms up" as an idiom for drinking; in another sense, we can lift what are often thought lowly of or overlooked—like the importance of mental health—to others' attention.

Written and designed by BGL.Signal, "Bottoms Up" is an interactive fiction describing eye-opening fear and dual diagnosis through the eyes of one girl.

"Trigger Warnings for Trypophobia and Ommetaphobia.
Disclaimer: the creators do not have first-hand experience of, nor diagnosed with dual diagnosis or phobias implied in the story, but have done sufficient research in order to grasp understandings.
Use the left and right arrow keys to navigate.
Best viewed with a resolution of 1920 x 1080."
