When Alone

Master Siopao and Co.

In the end, are we really all alone?

Master Siopao and Co. capture phobias in "When Alone," a cinematographic experience traveling to and through the sonder of lives in the ripple effect brought about by different fears.

Phobia tackled: Monophobia - fear of being alone
To raise awareness for the existence of the different kinds of phobias and how they affect the lives of people.
