Maybe then I'll find you in a sidewalk?

Team Rocket, in gorgeous amateur cinematography and sound is a film on the struggles of teenagers of different backgrounds -- each facing their own battles and condemning themselves to different fates.

It shows life difference between financially secure kids and those who aren't. Parent's destroying the dreams and creativity when time comes to college and jobs. Becoming worried of not having money for college or not being accepted to school. Parents not having time for their children. Suicidal thoughts of every teenager. *We didn't have enough time to finish all the scenes.
