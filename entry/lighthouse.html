Family Issues Website

Lighthouse

Bianca Aguilar

Is the invasion of privacy just when it comes to security?

"Lighthouse" is a website made by Bianca Aguilar for a hypothetical security software which scans a family's devices for underlying secrets.

"No notable trigger warnings
Is the invasion of privacy just when it comes to safety? This question has become a longstanding debate, with both parents and children voicing their opinions on the issue. I wanted to explore both sides of this concept in Lighthouse, which is a website for a hypothetical software. A ""what if?"" materialized. Yes, you can discover underlying issues in the family if this privacy were invaded, but this invasion creates another issue in itself. If an immoral action has good intentions, it still lacks morals."
