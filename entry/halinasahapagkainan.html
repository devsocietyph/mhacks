Eating Disorders Interactive Fiction

Halina sa Hapagkainan

Paper Crane

It tightens. It tightens. And it tightens.

"Halina sa Hapagkainan" is a memoir on eating disorders, the crunch and twist of a body given up in growth, self-hatred, and loss of mind. A special award winner, this interactive fiction is a confessional vow towards the Filipino mental health struggle.

"Trigger warnings can also be found in the first page of my work. I wrote, ""Warning: The topic I worked on for MHacks17 is eating disorders. This work tackles issues regarding body image, unhealthy eating habits. There is a description of a panic attack and mentions of suicidal thoughts."" "
