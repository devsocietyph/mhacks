Depression Illustration

Loneliness, Anxiety, Guilt

Aaron Natividad

"I scream at the void, afraid of monsters I cannot see."

Drawn by Aaron Natividad, "Symptoms of Depression" is an illustration set composed of "Loneliness", "Anxiety", and "Guilt". Done in black and white, the subjects are done in a monstrous form to depict the harrowing effects of the symptoms.

The Illustrations represent one symptom of depression (or reason, depending on the perspective), more should've been included yet it wasn't possible due to time constraints and other work.
